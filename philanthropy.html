Philanthropy

Tau Kappa Epsilon

Tau Kappa Epsilon is a non-profit organization that holds many philanthropic events. Within Tau Kappa Epsilon, I have experienced many volunteer opportunities that has involved me within my community, and memories that have help shape me into who I am today. Tau Kappa Epsilon has showed me the importance of Philanthropy and how to make a impact.

St. Jude Children's Research Hospital

St. Jude is Tau Kappa Epsilon's partner charity, and as a member in TKE, I have had the opportunity to fundraise and participate in multiple St. Jude events. I am a yearly volunteer for the Atlanta St. Jude walk and have participated 2 years in a row with current plans for next year.

Carroll County Mental Health Association

Within Tau Kappa Epsilon, I have also done additional community work with the CMHA in order to help out with problems more local to us at West Georgia. We volunteer to support their yearly masquerade ball fundraiser, and offer a helping hand with any help they need.
